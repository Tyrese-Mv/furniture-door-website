The House of ANASA

A door is the first thing a room says. We make it say everything.

ANASA builds interior doors from multi-layer solid walnut wood around a dense graphite core. The result has real presence: quiet between rooms, substantial in the hand, and dimensionally stable for a lifetime. Luxury, for us, is not decoration. It is the way a door feels when it closes.

Questions

Before you enquire.

Can doors be made to custom sizes?

Yes. Every leaf and frame is measured and made to your exact opening — there are no standard sizes to fit around.

Do you install?

Yes. Our own fitters hang every door flush, concealed and precisely aligned.

Which finishes are available?

Light Oak, Walnut, Mahogany and Graphite, each with optional engraved geometric detailing.

How quiet is an ANASA door?

The dense graphite core absorbs sound, giving real acoustic privacy from one room to the next.

How do I get a price?

Send an enquiry with your space and rough sizes. Because every door is made to measure, pricing follows a short consultation.
