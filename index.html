The House of ANASA

A door is the first thing a room says. We make it say everything.

ANASA builds interior doors from multi-layer solid wood around a dense graphite core. The result has real presence: quiet between rooms, substantial in the hand, and dimensionally stable for a lifetime. Luxury, for us, is not decoration. It is the way a door feels when it closes.
